About

Rebecca leads the Production R&D subprogram within the Macroalgal Biofuels and Bioproducts project. This program works to select and cultivate high productivity species and strains of marine and freshwater macroalgae. It aims to identify environmental tolerances of these species and optimise biomass productivities through control of environment and inputs. The overarching goal is to provide a series of unique, high-performance macroalgal strains for use in biofuel and bioproducts applications. This program will also develop DNA barcoding techniques to identify cultivated strains of marine and freshwater macroalgae.
