Research Interests

Dr Dorothy Steane is a CRN* senior research fellow, based at the University of Tasmania.  She has over 20 years' experience developing and using DNA markers for plant systematics research.  Her current research program focuses on population genetics, phylogeny, evolution, adaptation and biogeography of Eucalyptus.  Dorothy also participates in numerous collaborative research projects that address taxonomic or evolutionary questions in other plant groups including Casuarinaceae, Clerodendrum (Lamiaceae), Dracophyllum (Ericaceae), Correa (Rutaceae) and Pterostylis (Orchidaceae).
