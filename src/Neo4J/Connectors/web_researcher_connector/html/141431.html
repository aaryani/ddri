University Research Themes

Biodiversity, ecology and conservation.

Research Interests

My current research projects include:

  • the effects of traffic noise on acoustic communication in birds and frogs
  • guidelines for strategic impact assessments under the EPBC Act
  • the value and impact of ecological field research
  • ecology and conservation of the growling grass frog Litoria raniformis on Melbourne's urban fringe
  • experimental translocation of tadpoles for conservation of frogs in urban habitats.

Research Group

  • Quantitative & Applied Ecology Group
