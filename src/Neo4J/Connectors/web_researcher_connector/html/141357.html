How does the activity of neural populations ultimately result in the 3D phenomenon we call 'perception'? My research uses a variety of techniques including functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI), psychophysics, eye movement tracking and computational modelling to understand the neural mechanisms of visual perception. I am also interested in using these techniques to both track the progression, and enable a better understanding, of certain neurological and ophthalmological conditions. I am currently working as a postdoctoral researcher in the laboratory of Prof. Colin Clifford.
