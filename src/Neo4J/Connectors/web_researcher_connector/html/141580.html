Areas of interest


I am currently an associate lecturer and postdoctoral researcher at the University of Sydney working with Dr. Nathan Lo. We are examining genetic caste and sex determination in termites. Check Nate’s website for more details.

During and subsequent to my graduate studies, my work has focused on incorporating molecular genetics into evolutionary ecology and conservation biology. I am a strong advocate in using multidisciplinary approaches in order to address crucial scientific topics, and therefore my research interest is quite broad:

Genetic sex and caste determination in termites
Conservation and population genetics
Host –parasite interaction
Ageing and immunology (immunosenescence)
