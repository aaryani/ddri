BSc (Hons 1st class)

Contact details for Ms Ashlee Shipham ashlee.shipham@griffithuni.edu.au

Thesis

Genetic and phenotypic variation in a rosella hybrid zone 

Description

Hybrid zones are spectacular in that they can provide grand insights into evolutionary processes. My research focuses on species within the genus Platycercus (Rosellas), and has a particular emphasis on the hybrid zone between the eastern rosella (P. eximius) and pale-headed rosella (P. adscitus). Using molecular methods I will be investigating factors maintaining the hybrid zone, and will attempt to identify genes under selection related to plumage and other morphological traits of interest. In addition to this, I will also examine the evolutionary relationships between members of this genus.
